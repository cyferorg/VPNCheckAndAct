VPN Check and Act Privacy Statement

No personal information is processed or collected. In fact, no information whatsoever leaves your computer/device. The only information held and used is the information (VPN names, tests and processes to terminate) the user enters through the application's user interface. This information is held locally on the computer/device. All the information generated during the running of the application is visible to the user in the log which is displayed on the application's main page. This information is deleted when the application is terminated.
